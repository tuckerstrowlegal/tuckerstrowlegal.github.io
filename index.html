Frequently Asked Questions

What information do you need to begin?

Complaint or petition, basic facts, employment history, wage/benefit detail, and any medical/vocational evaluations available.

How are assumptions handled?

All key assumptions (growth, discounting, mitigation) are stated explicitly with source citations and sensitivity ranges as needed.

Do you work on both plaintiff and defense matters?

Yes. Analyses are neutral and designed to be understood by judges and juries regardless of party.

What are typical timelines?

Drafts are commonly delivered within agreed timelines based on discovery and data availability. Rush options may be available.
